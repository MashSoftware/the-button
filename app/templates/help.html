Frequently asked questions

  • Why limit to 80 entries per account? Why not 50, or 100?

    • 80 entries will give most people about two months worth of history, based on someone recording two entries per day, five days a week. Less than two months history doesn't feel like enough, and more than this starts to impact on the storage requirements and cost of running the service.

  • What happens if I create a new entry after getting to the 80 limit?

    • When you click on "start now" the oldest entry you have made will be automatically deleted and the new entry created.
